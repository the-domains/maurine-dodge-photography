Maurine Dodge Photography

Confessions of a "Natural"

This book was put together with genealogists and quilters in mind. 80 pages of photos of quilts by Connie and Maurine Dodge and a few quilts made by a few of their ancestors. This book is also the fund raiser for the author's needed sight restoration and the continued care needed for the author's Mother who is living with stage four cancer.
